My name is Carl and I'm a developer, designer, and photographer currently living in Toronto. I'm a creator by heart that's passionate about everything I make whether that's taking photos of the scenic cityscape of Toronto, creating web pages for friends and clients, or designing apps (for fun or just redesigning something existing).

I've had the opportunity to work with companies like NRG Home Solar, Ryerson University, The Sake Institue of Ontario, London Family Court Clinic, and Propeller Ideas.

What am I good at?

Photography

Seeing the world behind glass and creating art with it is one of my biggest passions. To capture an image that would otherwise be glossed over is what excites me most.

I shoot mostly landscapes, and portraiture but I also have done several event shoots at the Kampai Sake Festival, Ryerson's Alumni Weekend, and the DOTA 2 International viewing at TIFF.

Web Development

As a seasoned front-end developer, I create experiences that pushes my skills to create a product that anyone would be proud to use.

I'm proficient in web technologies like HTML5, CSS3, CSS-Preprocessors (SASS), Javascript task runners (Gulp.js), Wordpress, ES6, ES5, VueJS, Vuex, Webpack, CSS methodology BEM/SMACSS, etc.

UI/UX Design

I love to make things look pretty while at the same time solving the everlasting problem of design. With the use of well thought out layouts, graphical elements, meaningful motion, and user stories of how a user would interact with the product, I can get reach closer to that perfect user experience.
